Chakrarahs

The belly button chakra, also known as the Manipura Chakra, is the third energy center in the body’s chakra system, located around the navel or solar plexus. It is associated with personal power, confidence, digestion, and overall vitality.

Represented by the color yellow and the element fire, this chakra governs transformation, ambition, and self-motivation. When balanced, it promotes a strong sense of self-worth, emotional stability, and a healthy digestive system, allowing individuals to feel empowered and in control of their lives.

Strengthening the navel chakra through meditation, breathwork, yoga, and nourishing practices— such as applying oil to the belly button—can help restore energy flow, enhance digestion, and boost inner confidence.
