Our Story

On a chilly autumn evening in Toronto, Kelly and Alina, both 22, found themselves sharing an Uber. Strangers at first, they quickly connected over their love for wellness, health, and self-care. What started as casual small talk soon turned into deep conversations about life, goals, and their shared passion for living a balanced, healthy lifestyle.

Over time, their friendship grew, filled with yoga sessions, smoothie dates, and late-night chats about everything from skincare routines to holistic healing.

Months later, during one of their usual catch-ups, they realized that their passion for wellness could be more than just a shared interest—it could be a business. Both frustrated by the lack of natural, high-quality wellness products, they saw an opportunity to create something meaningful. With Kelly’s business mindset and Alina’s expertise in nutrition and skincare, they began developing a line of belly button oils infused with Ayurvedic and Western ingredients.

What started as a simple friendship blossomed into a thriving business, built on their shared belief in self-care, sustainability, and empowering others to live healthier lives.
