Pechoti Method Explained

The Pechoti Method is an ancient Ayurvedic practice that involves applying essential oils to the navel (belly button). The belief is that the navel, being connected to over 72,000 nerve endings and pathways in the body, acts as a central point for absorbing nutrients and delivering their benefits throughout the system.

This method is said to promote relaxation, balance, and overall well-being, making it a gentle yet powerful self-care ritual.
