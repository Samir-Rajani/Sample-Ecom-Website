Your Guide to Using Belly Button Oils

Transform your evening routine with these simple steps:

  1. Gather your preferred Belly Button Oil in a sterilized amber glass bottle with a dropper.
  2. Shake well before each use.
  3. Apply 4-5 drops to cleansed, slightly damp skin around the navel every evening.
  4. Gently massage in upward motions to aid absorption.
  5. Let the oil work overnight to nourish, heal, and rejuvenate.
